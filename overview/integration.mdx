## Merchant Account Overview

![Log-in](/images/Signindark.jpg)

Your Merchant Account is essentially the “home base” for managing your partnership with Easepay. You will be able to use your dashboard to view or download payment activity, manage settlements, withdrawal request, issue refunds, generate payment invoices, and more.
