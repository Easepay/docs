---
title: 'Managed Account'
description: Our multi-tenancy account system allows you to create and manage fully seperate Easepay accounts on behalf of your platform's users in a completely transparent way.
---
>
As an Admin, you can create different Easepay account for different usecases allowing you to easily onboard your users onto Easepay platform while keeping full control of the user experience. 
>
> A managed account on Easepay is only visible to the account owner, keeping you in control of all user interactions.
